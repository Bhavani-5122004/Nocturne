PURPOSE

Our website, Nocturne, is a website containing a song database that aims at simplifying the process of finding and listening to music. We aim at providing music and songs of various artists, genres and languages to immerse our users in a a diverse music listening experience. This website allows users to choose songs from various artists and genres that suit their preferences.

WHAT OUR WEBSITE CONTAINS

Our website contains a home page that diplays top artists, albums, and songs. It contains an artists page that displays the various artists whose songs are on the website. The albums page of each artist has all the albums of each artist and each album has a corresponding songs page that displays all the songs in that album.

HOW THIS WEBSITE CAN BE USEFUL

As this website contains data about the songs and albums of various artists, it allows the user to not only choose and view the songs of their choice, but also exposes them and allows them to discover and explore new and diverse music of different genres and languages. They can navigate between the multiple artists and albums pages with the help of both the side navigation bar and the navigation bar on the top. The home page also allows the user to view the top artists, albums and songs to give them an insight into the trending artists and songs which may also allow them to choose new songs with ease.
